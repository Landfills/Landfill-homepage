What is Edgemere Landfill?

Edgemere Landfill, a former municipal dump in the Rockaways, Queens, has been closed for over 20 years. Today, Edgemere landfill is capped and is at the end of remedia­tion process. The land is facing an as-yet-undeter­mined future.
